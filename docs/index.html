full_chaos Reward Curve

Policy reward over the final curriculum stage (full_chaos): 5 debris bodies, 5 scatter shrapnel, ceiling crumble, fire zones, and wall collapse all active simultaneously.

Curriculum

Four progressive stages, each loading weights from the previous stage. Hazards are introduced incrementally so the policy first learns navigation, then avoidance.

Stage Name Primary Debris Scatter Crumble Steps End Reward
1 static_only 0 0 — 600k +8.5
2 debris_only 3 0 — 1.0M −2.8
3 debris_and_scatter 5 3 — 1.2M +0.17
4 full_chaos 5 5 ✓ 1.5M −10.9

Physics method: MuJoCo weld equality constraints are released at runtime (eq_active[i]=0), causing 180–420 kg concrete slabs to fall under gravity. 9 weld constraints total, triggered by elapsed steps or agent x-position.

Observation Space · 71-dim

No RGB camera. Policy navigates purely from position, velocity, and proximity sensors — analogous to LIDAR/sonar. Directly applicable to assistive devices for visually impaired navigation.
